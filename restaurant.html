OUR RESTAURANT

Our Hotel Restaurant offers its guests an unforgettable, unique, and incomparable dining experience. We serve delicious continental & African cuisines with all our fresh fruit and vegetables sourced directly from our main farm at kinna and the nearby market. With our exclusive fine dining area separate from the open restaurant, you will enjoy a magical experience custom arranged to your taste.

Our restaurant has the capacity to serve 60+ guests on the go and that makes us an ideal choice of location to host huge conferences.

For outside catering, we do not compromise on quality and we can deliver a restaurant feel to any location and comfortably serve guests on location.
