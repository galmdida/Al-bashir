Al-bashir Hotel

Al-bashir is a tranquil & inclusive 2-star superior hotel located in the heart of Kinna town yet allows a peaceful polished retreat among the buzz of the ever-vibrant town. We have 5 accommodation options ranging from deluxe rooms to triple rooms that are guaranteed to impress every discerning traveller.

Our main restaurant serves delicious continental & African cuisines with a capacity to serve 60+ guests within the huge elegant restaurant. This makes us an ideal location to host huge conferences; Get in touch with our reservation team for booking requests or book online via our website.
