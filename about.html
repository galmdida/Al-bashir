Al-Bashir Hotel, Kinna - Isiolo

Welcome to Al-Bashir, a contemporary style hotel that provides all the comfort, convenience and efficiency to the discerning visitors. Ideally located along Kinna-Maua road in Kinna, Al-Bashir Hotel Kinna features 48 well-appointed rooms, three meeting & conference rooms, restaurant, wellness center among other top facilities offering the best choice venue for your Business and Leisure stay.

Our Mission

To acquire the loyalty of our clients by exceeding their expectations in offering comfort, convenience and efficient services.

Vision

To be the preferred hotel recognized for providing world class affordable hospitality.

Core Values

Exceptional service, Integrity, Respect, Teamwork & Passion and Continuous Improvement.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Al-Bashir Hotel is affiliated with the Bilal Foundation. The Hotel and the Foundation continuously support various CSR initiative as a way of giving back to society, in line with the companies’ Sustainable Development Goals.

To participate in any of noble causes, contact us or reach out to the Bilal Foundation Trust and we will be more than happy to provide further details and information.

Al-Bashir Hotel Enviromental Policy

In tandem with our vision “To acquire the loyalty of our clients by exceeding their expectations in offering comfort, convenience and efficient services.” we are committed to continually improving the environmental that we operate in as well as ensuring social sustainability. Al-bashir Hotel identifies and refines existing policies clearly following the standards, legislation and bench-marking of third-party authorities.
